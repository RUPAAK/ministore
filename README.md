# Deleting Your Memore Account

To request deletion of your Ministore account and all associated data, please submit the following information via our Google Form: [https://forms.gle/pRoiggnX2EkpFdfP9]

### Data that will be deleted:
- Products listing, Orders
- Product images
- Account credentials (store name, email)

We will process deletion requests within 7 days. Any data retained for legal or backup purposes will be anonymized and deleted within 30 days.
